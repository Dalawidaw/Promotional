College of Information and Computer Studies (CICS)

About

The College of Information and Computer Studies (CICS), we are committed to providing students with top-notch education and hands-on experience in the fields of Information Technology and Computer Science. Our diverse array of programs, including Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT), Bachelor of Library and Information Science (BLIS), and Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (COMSCI), are designed to equip you with the essential knowledge and skills to succeed in today’s fast-paced digital world. Whether you're interested in building innovative software, managing information systems, or shaping the future of digital libraries, CICS offers a comprehensive curriculum, expert faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities to help you reach your academic and professional goals. Join us and be part of a dynamic community dedicated to learning, growth, and technology. Explore our courses, learn about our admissions process, and discover how CICS can pave the way for your future success!

Mission

Human resources development through quality and relevant education, environment-friendly mordern technologies and preservation of Filipino values and culture for sustainable and improved quality of life.

Vision

A globally competitive State University in Asia.

Goals

The CICS endeavors to: train students to become computer professionals knowledgeable and competent in meeting the growing demand for software developers and IT persons in government, business, industry and education; harness and undertake relevant ICT researches to provide valuable services and to share expertise and facilities with various stakeholders on the transfer and promotion of information and communications technology for local, regional and national benefit; and inculcate in its students positive Filipino values and attitudes as well as the value of independent and life-long learning.
